b element: bold text
strong element: text with strong importance, typically displayed in bold
i element: text in an alternate voice or mood, typically displayed in italic
em element: emphasized text, typically displayed in italic
small element: smaller text
mark element: text that should be marked or highlighted
del element: text that has been deleted, usually with a strikethrough
ins element: text that has been inserted, typically underlined
sub element: subscript text, appears half a char below the normal line
sup element: superscript text, appears half a char abovethe normal line
